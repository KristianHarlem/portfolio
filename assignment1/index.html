About me

Being bilingual in Norwegian and English opens doors to diverse opportunities and connections, both personally and professionally. In the realm of literature, I find myself drawn to fiction, particularly the realms of sci-fi and fantasy, where imagination knows no bounds.

While music isn't a top priority for me, I do appreciate its ability to soothe and uplift. However, I find my true sanctuary indoors, where I can unwind and recharge surrounded by the comforts of home.

And speaking of home, there's nothing quite like the aroma of a home-cooked meal wafting through the air. As a self-proclaimed night owl, mornings are not my forte, but I make up for it with my enthusiasm for the culinary arts and the adventures that each day brings.

So, whether it's delving into a new sci-fi novel, whipping up a culinary masterpiece, or embarking on a virtual quest, I'm always ready to embrace the journey with curiosity and an open mind.
